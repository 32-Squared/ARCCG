How to Play
✕

Object

Be the first player to move 3 Vehicles through all 4 Racing Realms.

Turn Sequence

  1. Draw — draw 1 card (auto)
  2. Advance — vehicles meeting escape values move forward (auto)
  3. Play Vehicle — place 1 vehicle from hand into Realm 1 for free
  4. Tune Up — terrain bonuses & token effects resolve (auto)
  5. Action Phase — spend APs to equip cards & play hazards
  6. End Turn — discard to 7 cards, pass to opponent

SPP — Speed · Power · Performance

Each realm has one Escape Value your vehicle's matching stat must meet or beat to advance. SPPf — equip Mods, Shifts and Accelechargers to boost your totals.

Action Points (APs)

You start each turn with 3 APs. Having 2+ vehicles from the same team in play gives +1 AP per team. Spend APs to play Mods (permanent), Shifts (until advance), Accelechargers (until advance, 1 per vehicle), and Hazards (target opponent).

Modability

Mods can only be equipped on vehicles with a matching icon: Street, Race, or Off-Road. Some cards say "Modability rules DO NOT apply" — those can equip any Mod.

Async Multiplayer

After your turn, tap Share to copy a link. Send it to your opponent via WhatsApp, iMessage etc. They open the link and take their turn. The entire game state is in the URL.

Controls

  • Tap a hand card to select it, then tap a vehicle stack or realm to act
  • Tap a selected card again to deselect
  • Long-press / right-click any card to see its full details
  • Pinch to zoom on supported devices
