How to Play
✕

Object

Be the first player to move 3 Vehicles through all 4 Racing Realms.

Turn Sequence

  1. Draw — auto
  2. Advance — vehicles meeting escape values move forward (auto)
  3. Play Vehicle — place 1 vehicle from hand into Realm 1 for free
  4. Tune Up — terrain bonuses & token effects resolve (auto)
  5. Action Phase — spend APs to equip cards & play hazards
  6. End Turn — discard to 7 cards, pass to opponent

SPP — Speed · Power · Performance

Each realm has one Escape Value your vehicle's matching stat must meet or beat. Equip Mods, Shifts and Accelechargers to boost totals.

Action Points

Start with 3 APs. Having 2+ vehicles of the same team in play gives +1 AP per team.

Async Multiplayer

After your turn, tap Share. Your opponent opens that link and takes their turn. The full game state is in the URL.

Controls

  • Tap a hand card to select, then tap a vehicle to act
  • Tap selected card again to deselect
  • Long-press / right-click any card for full details
  • Tap the logo or Menu to return to main menu
